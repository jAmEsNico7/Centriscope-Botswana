About Us

About Us


CentriScope is a medical consumables and equipment company dedicated to providing the absolute best of everything we have to offer. Our driving force is passion; the passion to provide our customers and clients with up-to-date, cutting edge, dynamic medical technology and supplies. We provide a high quality, high reliability offering across the board and our client-centric approach means we are only happy when the customer is happy. Our affiliations and partnerships with globally-acclaimed, reputable brands in the medical space are our truest currency. These links aid us in being at the epicenter of a vast global network that bridges gaps and fills voids in clinics, hospitals, hospices, research centers and the medical industry as a whole.

Our Services

Laboratory

CentriScope is a pioneer in offering lab solutions for a multitude of applications. Be it for diagnostic applications or for research purposes, we are who you are looking for. With the founder’s combined expertise of over 10 years, we have established a lattice with esteemed manufacturers from all over the world to provide a top notch offering. We provide our clients with specialized, high-quality solutions everytime.

Health Care

Our broad catalogue ensures product consolidation for our valued customers. Our wide range of healthcare solutions consist of but are not limited to infection control consumables, patient monitoring equipment, respiratory assistance, emergency support, and homecare equipment.

Instruments&Machinery

CentriScope provides complete turnkey solutions in liquid analysis and measurements, water purification systems, dosing, and metering systems in collaboration with some of the world's top automation and instrumentation companies. We offer solutions to the food and beverage, water and wastewater, mining, agricultural, and power generation industries.
