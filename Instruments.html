Autoclaves & Sterilizers


The extensive selection of autoclaves, also known as steam sterilizers and utilized frequently in the healthcare or industrial sectors, is offered by Aufbau. A device known as an autoclave uses steam under pressure to destroy dangerous bacteria, viruses, fungus, and spores on objects that are placed inside a pressure vessel at temperatures between 121° C (250° F) and 135° C (275° F).
